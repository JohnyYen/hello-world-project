# Backgrounds Summary for Hello World Game

This document lists all the backgrounds used in the game, the dialogues where they appear, and prompts for generating these backgrounds.

## Backgrounds List

### 1. Building Entrance Background
- **File Path**: `backgrounds/chapter_1/building_entrance.png`
- **Dialogues**: C00_E01_Entrada_Facultad.dialogue
- **Description**: The entrance to the faculty building where the player arrives for the first time
- **Generation Prompt**: A welcoming university building entrance during daytime with students and professors walking around, architecture that conveys academic prestige, with trees and pathways in front, and a sense of new beginnings and opportunity

### 2. Theater Full Background
- **File Path**: `backgrounds/chapter_1/theater_full.png`
- **Dialogues**: C00_E03_Teatro_Facultad.dialogue
- **Description**: The interior of a full theater where the welcome speech from the dean takes place
- **Generation Prompt**: A large academic theater filled with new students attending the dean's welcome speech, with a stage where the dean and Orqui's hologram are visible, warm lighting, an atmosphere of inspiration and academic ceremony

### 3. Hallway Background
- **File Path**: `backgrounds/chapter_1/hallway.png`

## Summary of Background Usage

Some backgrounds are used in multiple contexts:

- **Dorm Room Night** appears in both C00_E05_Llegada_Dormitorio.dialogue and Tutorial_Room_Finish.dialogue, serving as a restful and contemplative setting.

This collection of backgrounds supports the narrative journey of the student character from arrival at the university to their first days of classes, covering all major settings within the academic environment.
